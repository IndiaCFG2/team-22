Our Program

Holistic Child Development

The School Readiness Program aims to create conducive learning environments at school and at home by creating empowered parents, equipped teachers and enriched classrooms to ensure holistic development of each child

Enriched Classrooms

We provide schools with comprehensive early childhood kit comprising fun, safe durable learning materials and age appropriate student workbooks.

Equipped Teachers

We have a year long teacher training and program support model to ensure developmentally appropriate practices in classroom.

Empowered Parents

We work with parents to build awareness on helping children better in their home environment through targeted workshops and home activity sheets

Making every child school ready

We, Key Education Foundation,as a non profit team try to make every child school ready by focussing on things like
Creativity and Curiosity: A theme based approach in the curriculum to encourage curiosity and build aesthetic sense in children is used
Early Numeracy: Materials and cognitive activities to promote pre-math skills and number sense
Social and Emotional Development : Circle time and storytelling to build essential social and emotional skills in children
Early Literacy: We provide materials and activities that focus on listening, speaking, reading and writing skills
Physical Development: Materials and activities to ensure fine and gross motor development which is crucial in the early years
